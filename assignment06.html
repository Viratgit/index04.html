Recipe of the Day: Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Chicken 1 kg
Basmati Rice 500 g
Yogurt 200 g
Spices To taste
Onions 3 large, sliced
Tomatoes 2 large, chopped

Instructions

  1. Wash the basmati rice and soak it for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices. Let it sit for 1 hour.
  3. Fry onions until golden brown. Set aside half for garnishing.
  4. Add marinated chicken to the remaining onions and cook until tender.
  5. Cook the soaked rice in boiling water until 70% done. Drain the water.
  6. Layer the chicken and rice in a pot. Add fried onions on top.
  7. Seal the pot and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-30 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with raita or salad.
